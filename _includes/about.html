About me

Rand Xie loves mathematics, programming, writing, speaking, tennis and table-tennis. In his spare time, he plays data challenges, contributes to open source and researches on different topics including machine learning, Bayesian statistics, control and estimation theory. He is currently a Software Engineer in Machine Learning at Google Cloud AI. Previously, he was a Data Scientist at Uptake, building an internal machine learning framework for industrial time series. Before joining Uptake, he worked as a software engineer at MathWorks and was one of the core developers in predictive maintenance toolbox. Before becoming a software engineer and data scientist, Rand was a mechanical engineer and keen on learning everything about manufacturing and aeronautics.
